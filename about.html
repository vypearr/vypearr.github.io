A little more about me

My work spans embedded systems and software. I enjoy the full process: breaking a problem down, experimenting with the details, and turning an early idea into something people can actually use.

Projects like my Light Chaser and Arduino game let me work close to the hardware—with timers, interrupts, inputs, and displays. BookieBets pushed me into backend logic, state, and building interactive experiences for a community.

Across both worlds, the common thread is curiosity. I care about clear logic, responsive experiences, and learning by making.
